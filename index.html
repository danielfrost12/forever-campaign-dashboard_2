What Each Test Tells a Manager

🎯Engagement Test (ThruPlays)
Question: Can we build cheap awareness at scale?
Answer: Yes — overwhelmingly. At $0.002/ThruPlay, we're getting 500 video views per dollar. This is the top-of-funnel awareness engine that primes audiences for retargeting.
For a manager: This proves the creative resonates. The ad isn't just being shown — people are watching it through. That's the hardest part to buy.
🧪Age/Creative Test (Landing Page Views)
Question: Which audience and creative combo drives the cheapest qualified traffic?
Answer: Age 35-44 with age-targeted creative wins at $0.04/LPV. Music-interest targeting is slightly less efficient. The 25-34 demo brings volume but at higher cost.
For a manager: This is the optimization layer. Instead of guessing which audience, we ran 4 parallel tests with controlled variables. Now we know exactly where to allocate.
🔥Spotify Cold Traffic (BR)
Question: Can we profitably drive streams from cold audiences?
Answer: At $0.11/click, each Spotify landing costs about a dime. With 3,402 streams and Brazil representing 65% of listens, the pipeline is converting. Algorithm already driving 14% of streams.
For a manager: This is the business case. Paid click → Spotify stream → algorithm pickup → organic growth. We're at the inflection point where paid is seeding organic.

Next Steps & Scaling Plan

Phase 1 (Complete — Week 1): Testing phase complete. Winners identified: BR Cold for Spotify clicks, Age 25/34 for LPV volume, Engagement for awareness. $754 of $5K deployed.

Phase 2 (Current — Weeks 2–3): Scale winning ad sets. BR Cold driving 98% of Spotify clicks. Algorithmic already at 14% and climbing. Consolidate budget into proven funnels.

Phase 3 (Weeks 3–4): Full scaling with $4,246 remaining. As algorithmic % increases, reduce paid spend and let organic compound. Target: 10K+ streams by day 30.

The framework is designed so every dollar spent generates data, not just streams. That data compounds into better targeting, which compounds into cheaper streams.
